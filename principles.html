Our Principles

La Liga is founded on five key principles:

  • Ideas Belong to Everyone - Knowledge should be shared, not sold.
  • Money Comes from Work - Wealth should come from productive work.
  • Equality for All - Everyone deserves equal treatment.
  • Fair Inheritance - Wealth should be distributed fairly, not concentrated.
  • No to War Profits - We oppose making money from the weapons industry.
